MUSIC

Music is the art of combining sounds in a way that expresses, in an orderly and pleasing manner to the ear, moods, images, and ideal aspirations.

It emerged as a need to reinforce language and to accompany forms of worship and theatrical representations. The ancients attributed it to divine origin and held it in great esteem, especially the Greeks, with whom it was closely connected to poetry and dance.

Generally, music is classified into:

  • Classical Music
  • Pop Music
  • Vocal Music
