CLASSICAL MUSIC

Classical music is the collection of musical combinations that, due to their simplicity of structure, balance, order, and proportion, are considered models of the genre.

Classical music was renewed at the turn of the two centuries by some great composers, such as the Russian Tchaikovsky, whose extraordinary "Piano Concerto No. 1" you are currently listening to in the background.
