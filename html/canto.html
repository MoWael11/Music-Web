VOCAL MUSIC

Music has often been accompanied by the human voice, forming the combination of sounds called singing, which includes a vast range of genres, from religious choirs to compositions by modern singer-songwriters.

Italy has made a significant contribution to the development of singing with its great composers such as Rossini, Verdi, Puccini, and many others. For this reason, our country is considered around the world as "the homeland of bel canto."

In the background, you can listen to the music of SUO GAN, an ancient Gaelic song also used by director Spielberg as the theme of the film "Empire of the Sun."
